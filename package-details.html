📅 Detailed Itinerary

1
Arrival in Tokyo
Welcome to Japan! Airport pickup and transfer to your hotel in Shibuya. Evening welcome dinner featuring authentic Japanese cuisine and trip orientation with your local guide.
2
Tokyo Exploration
Visit Senso-ji Temple, explore the Imperial Palace East Gardens, and experience the vibrant Harajuku district. Traditional kaiseki dinner at a renowned restaurant.
3
Mount Fuji Day Trip
Full-day excursion to Hakone National Park with views of Mount Fuji. Lake Ashi cruise, cable car rides, and traditional onsen (hot spring) experience.
4
Tokyo to Kyoto
Experience the famous bullet train (Shinkansen) to Kyoto. Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine with its thousands of torii gates. Evening walking tour of the historic Gion district.
5
Kyoto Temples & Culture
Explore Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), walk through the magical Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, and participate in a traditional tea ceremony with a tea master.
6
Nara Day Trip
Visit Nara, Japan's first capital. Explore Todai-ji Temple with its giant Buddha statue, feed the sacred deer in Nara Park, and return to Kyoto for farewell dinner.
7
Osaka Castle & Cuisine
Travel to Osaka, visit the magnificent Osaka Castle, explore Dotonbori district, and enjoy a street food tour featuring takoyaki, okonomiyaki, and more.
8
Departure
Last-minute shopping in Osaka, airport transfer, and departure. Take home unforgettable memories of your Japanese cultural adventure.
