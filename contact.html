üó∫Ô∏è Trip Planning

How far in advance should I book my trip? +

For international trips, we recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for the best prices and availability. Domestic trips can be booked 4-6 weeks ahead. However, our AI can find great deals even for last-minute bookings!

Can I modify my AI-generated itinerary? +

Absolutely! All our itineraries are fully customizable. You can add, remove, or modify any activities, accommodations, or destinations. Our travel experts can help you make changes that fit your preferences perfectly.

Do you provide travel insurance recommendations? +

Yes! We partner with leading travel insurance providers to offer comprehensive coverage options. Our experts can recommend the best policy based on your destination, trip duration, and activities planned.

üí≥ Booking & Payments

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. All payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

What's your cancellation policy? +

Cancellation policies vary by service provider. Most hotels offer free cancellation up to 24-48 hours before check-in. Flights typically have different rules. We'll clearly show all cancellation terms before you book.

Can I pay in installments for expensive trips? +

Yes! For trips over $2,000, we offer flexible payment plans. You can split your payment into 2-4 installments with no additional fees. This makes luxury travel more accessible and helps with budgeting.

‚öôÔ∏è Technical Support

Why isn't the AI working on my device? +

Most AI issues are browser-related. Try clearing your cache, updating your browser, or switching to Chrome/Safari. If problems persist, our technical team can help via live chat.

How do I reset my account password? +

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send reset instructions. If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

üë§ Account & Profile

How do I update my travel preferences? +

Go to your Profile Settings and click "Travel Preferences". You can update your preferred destinations, budget range, travel style, and activity interests. This helps our AI create better recommendations.

Can I save multiple trip ideas? +

Yes! You can save unlimited trip ideas to your "My Trips" dashboard. Each saved trip includes your itinerary, bookings, and notes. Perfect for planning multiple vacations or comparing options.
